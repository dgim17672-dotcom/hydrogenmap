Privacy Policy – HydrogenMap

Last updated: May 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

HydrogenMap collects your approximate location (with permission) solely to show nearby hydrogen stations. We do not collect personal identifiers, sell data, or share it with third parties.

Location is used only in real time to find stations near you. It is not stored on our servers.

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most) to improve the app. No personal data is included.
